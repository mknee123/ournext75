What is the Summit?

As the culmination of three Regional Visioning Workshops, we’re inviting 1,000 leaders from across our region to a summit at the Wyndam Grand hotel in downtown Pittsburgh. Today’s and tomorrow’s leaders will compare notes on what they’re doing to move the region forward, find ways to better align their efforts, and commit to working together on transformational regional projects to take the Pittsburgh region into the future.

Special Guests:

Caterina Fake

Headlining the summit will be Keynote Speaker Caterina Fake: entrepreneur (co-founder of Flickr, founder of Findery and former chair of Etsy), investor, top-rated podcast host, “Silicon Valley pioneer” and Pittsburgh native – an all-around Renaissance woman. With an eye to Our Next 75, Caterina will energize summit attendees to unleash their creativity, embrace disruption and leverage innovation to create the kind of future we’ll collectively need and want to succeed and thrive.

Andy Levine

Joining the Keynote Speaker is Andy Levine, chairman of Development Counsellors International (DCI) – one of the country’s top economic development and place marketing enterprises. A leading economic development practitioner, Andy will leverage his expertise to guide regional leaders and doers toward finding the region’s sweet spots for the future in light of our unique strengths and what other places are thinking about or already doing to establish their own futures.
