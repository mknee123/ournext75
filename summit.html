What was the Summit?

As the culmination of three Regional Visioning Workshops, we invited 1,000+ leaders from across our region to a summit at the Wyndham Grand hotel in downtown Pittsburgh. Today’s and tomorrow’s leaders compared notes on what they’re doing to move the region forward, found way to better align their efforts, and committed to working together on transformational regional projects to take the Pittsburgh region into the future.

The Summit was a part of the multi-year agenda-setting process for the Allegheny Conference on Community Development. The Conference has held similar summits every few years for more than a decade, but this one is larger by far. For the 75th, we wanted to make it as diverse as possible by every measure you can think of -- to share ideas, chart a path forward, and do it together.

Photo Credit: J.R. Mankoff

Caterina Fake, Keynote Speaker

Caterina is an entrepreneur (co-founder of Flickr, founder of Findery and former chair of Etsy), investor, top-rated podcast host, “Silicon Valley pioneer” and Pittsburgh native – an all-around Renaissance woman. With an eye to Our Next 75, Caterina energized summit attendees to unleash their creativity, embrace disruption and leverage innovation to create the kind of future we’ll collectively need and want to succeed and thrive.

Andy Levine

Andy is the chairman of Development Counsellors International (DCI) – one of the country’s top economic development and place marketing enterprises. A leading economic development practitioner, Andy leveraged his expertise to guide regional leaders and doers toward finding the region’s sweet spots for the future in light of our unique strengths and what other places are thinking about or already doing to establish their own futures.
